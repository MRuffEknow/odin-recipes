Caesar Salad Recipe

Photo of Caesar Salad

Description

This salad has it's origins in Tijuana Mexico developed at the famous Caesar's restaurant.

Don't skip the anchovies as it adds the much needed saltiness to the dressing without the fishiness you might expect.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads of Romaine lettuce
  • 1/4 cup of shredded parmasean cheese
  • 1/4 cup of EVOO
  • 1 anchovy
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 Teaspoon of cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl mash the anchovy until it becomes a paste
  2. Mix inthe cheese, lemon juice, and black pepper
  3. Whisking vigourousy, add the EVOO slowly until the mixture emulsafies into a dressing
  4. Toss in lettuce and serve
