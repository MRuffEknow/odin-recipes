Cacio e Pepe Pasta Recipe

Photo of Cacio e Pepe Pasta

Description

This is a classic Roman dish that relies on 3 ingrediants: fresh pasta, good parmasean cheese, and cracked blacked pepper.

This dish comes together fast, so make sure you have your ingrediants ready to go

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Spaghetti pasta
  • 1/4 cup of shredded parmasean cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon of cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Start by bringing a pot to boil. Once boiling add pasta and cook until al dente
  2. Drain the pasta saving a cup of cooking water
  3. Add the pasta, cheese, and cracked pepper in a bowl
  4. Mix together adding cooking water as needed
